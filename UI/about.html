What is Book-A-Meal

Book-A-Meal is an application that allows customers to make food orders and helps the food vendor know what the customers want to eat. It accomodates both customers and caterers. A caterer can manage (i.e: add, modify and delete) meal options in the application.The caterer can set menu for a specific day by selecting from the meal options available on the system. Users can see the menu for a specific day and select an option from the menu. Caterer can see the summary of the orders for a specific day and details for all the orders . .
